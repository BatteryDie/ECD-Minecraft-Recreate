In a world where imagination knows no bounds, one dedicated group of alumni embarked on a remarkable journey. Their mission is to recreate a place where their dreams could thrive, where every brick, every texture, and every signpost has been carefully crafted to create an immersive experience that reflects the school's values of inclusivity, education, and community and celebrate the school's 60th anniversary.

Welcome to the E.C. Drury School for the Deaf, where in the realm of Minecraft, dreams become reality.

FAQ

Q: How long does this public Minecraft server last?
A: May 28th to July 3th, 2023 August 8, 2023.
Q: What do you after this public Minecraft server closed?
A: We are planning to archive the Minecraft world file and website to some digital archives and museums on internet like a time capsule. People can download and load the Minecraft world file in their own computer.
Q: Why can't I break or place block?
A: This campus is in view mode like a museum.
Q: Why are many room and building not accessible?
A: Some of them are restricted access. We do not have information/source enough. Secondary School, Sport Complex, and Cafeteria are accessible at this time.
Q: Why are there item frame behind the graphic such as sign and banner?
A: This is Bedrock Edition issue that it do not allow invisible item frame for technical reason. We highly recommend play Java Edition for better experience
Q: Why can't I play it in latest version of Minecraft?
A: Because most plugins in our server are not compatible with 1.20 or newer until the plugins developer updated them.

For Java Edition users, Please downgrade to Minecraft Java Edition 1.19.4. Click here to see step by step.

Bedrock Edition users cannot downgrade version if App Store don't have option.
